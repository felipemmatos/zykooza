Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

work4mob ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications and web services.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our apps or services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information from users unless it is voluntarily provided.

Our apps may collect limited technical and usage information through third-party services such as Firebase Analytics.

2. Firebase Analytics

Our apps may use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to understand app performance, user engagement, crashes, usage events, device type, app version, and general usage statistics.

Firebase Analytics helps us improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

We do not use Firebase Analytics to personally identify individual users.

3. Data Usage

We may use collected information to:

  • Improve app performance and stability.
  • Understand general usage trends.
  • Fix crashes and technical issues.
  • Improve user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell users' personal information.

Information collected through third-party services, such as Firebase Analytics, is handled according to the privacy policies of those providers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that collect information used to identify or analyze usage.

These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics, if enabled

https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our apps do not directly use cookies for tracking. However, third-party services may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies according to their own policies.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

We do not store personal information on our own servers unless clearly required for app functionality. Analytics data may be retained by Google/Firebase according to their own retention policies and settings.
